Lang / list / merge

Merges two List into one.

Syntax:
(List) [list merge:<(List) value_1>,<(List) value_2>]
Parameters:
value_1 Left operand value.
value_2 Right operand value.
Returns:

Merged List from value_1 and value_2.

Example:
in:
[list merge:[list:abc,def,ghi],[list:123,456,789]]
out:
[abc,def,ghi,123,456,789]
