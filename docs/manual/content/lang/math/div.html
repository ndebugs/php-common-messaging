Lang / math / div

Division operator.

Syntax:
(Number) [math div:<(Number) value_1>,<(Number) value_2>]
Parameters:
value_1 Left operand value.
value_2 Right operand value.
Returns:

Division operation between value_1 and value_2.

Example:
in:
[math div:5,2] [math div:5,2.0]
out:
2 2.5
