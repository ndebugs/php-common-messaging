Lang / math / max

Maximum Number selector.

Syntax:
(Number) [math max:<(Number) value_1>,<(Number) value_2>]
Parameters:
value_1 Left operand value.
value_2 Right operand value.
Returns:

Maximum value between value_1 and value_2.

Example:
in:
[math max:0,1] [math max:1,1]
out:
1 1
