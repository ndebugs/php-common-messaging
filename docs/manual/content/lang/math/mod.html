Lang / math / mod

Modulus operator.

Syntax:
(Number) [math mod:<(Number) value_1>,<(Number) value_2>]
Parameters:
value_1 Left operand value.
value_2 Right operand value.
Returns:

Modulus operation between value_1 and value_2.

Example:
in:
[math mod:5,2] [math mod:5,2.0]
out:
1 1.0
