Lang / math / mul

Multiplication operator.

Syntax:
(Number) [math mul:<(Number) value_1>,<(Number) value_2>]
Parameters:
value_1 Left operand value.
value_2 Right operand value.
Returns:

Multiplication operation between value_1 and value_2.

Example:
in:
[math mul:2,1] [math mul:2,1.0]
out:
2 2.0
