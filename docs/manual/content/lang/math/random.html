Random function.

Syntax:
(Number) [math random:<(Number) value_1>,<(Number) value_2>]
Parameters:
value_1 Minimum value.
value_2 Maximum value.
Returns:

Example:
in:
[between:[math random:1,10],1,10]
out:
true
